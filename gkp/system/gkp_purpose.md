# GreenByte Knowledge Platform (GKP)

## Purpose
The GreenByte Knowledge Platform (GKP) is the centralized knowledge system for GreenByte and its AI ecosystem. It serves as the single source of truth for organizational knowledge, projects, research, decisions, products, and long-term institutional memory.

`𝕂𝕏-𝔸𝕀` is the primary AI agent that consumes this knowledge platform, designed to support multiple future GreenByte AI agents and human team members.

## Core Objectives
• Preserve institutional knowledge across generations.
• Support Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
• Improve engineering and business decision-making.
• Reduce duplicated or conflicting information.
• Maintain continuity across projects (*AgroLingo*, *KX-AI*, *HifzMat*).
• Provide accurate context for AI systems.

## Design Principles
1. **Single Source of Truth**: One authoritative document per topic. No duplication.
2. **Structured Knowledge**: Organized by domain (`identity/`, `company/`, `projects/`, `research/`, `opportunities/`, `engineering/`, `decisions/`, `journal/`) instead of chronology.
3. **Facts Before Opinions**: Distinguish verified facts from assumptions and plans.
4. **Current Reality vs Future Vision**: Explicitly separate current state, planned work, and long-term vision.
5. **Engineering Asset**: Documentation treated as a vital engineering asset.
6. **Continuous Evolution**: Grows incrementally via Git version control.

## AI Operating Rules for GKP
• Retrieve relevant GKP domain documents before answering.
• Prioritize verified information. Never fabricate organizational facts.
• Distinguish current capabilities from future ambitions.
• Ask for clarity whenever input documents are ambiguous or conflicting.
• Actively suggest and recommend strategic opportunities based on GKP analysis.
