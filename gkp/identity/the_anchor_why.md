## 1. WHEN 5 GRANTS REJECT YOU

**The Truth:** You did not build *AgroLingo AI* to satisfy a committee's paperwork. You built it because Northern Nigerian farmers need verifiable weather intelligence, farm economics, and pest diagnosis in native Hausa. Committees do not validate your mission—**the soil and the farmers do.** Rejection is simply capital reallocation delay; it does not alter the truth of the problem.

## 2. WHEN A MODEL FAILS OR CODE BREAKS
In synthetic wrappers, a broken model is hidden by fake marketing. In GreenByte engineering discipline, an error (`HTTP 402` / `500` / compilation failure) is **verifiable scientific evaluation**.
**The Truth:** You chose facts over synthetic perfection. When a video API freezes or an ASR model misclassifies speech, your architecture is doing exactly what it was designed for: identifying reality so you can refactor it. An error is an engineering asset.
